<p align="justify">Pinclusive is a multiplayer wired and Bluetooth controller adapter for Pinball Machines, built on the popular ESP32 chip. It allows connection of multiple controllers and gamepads as listed below, keyboards and mice over bluetooth, as well as wired buttons designed for disabilities such as the Logitech Adaptive kit. Pinclusive supports up to 4 simultaneous devices, for multiplayer games !</p>
<p align="justify">Simply use the Bluetooth devices you already own!  Xbos Adaptive Controller (XAC), Quadstick, Wii, Switch, PS3, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S & generic HID Bluetooth (BR/EDR & LE) devices are supported.</p>
<p align="justify">The project is open source hardware & software under the CC BY-NC 4.0 & Apache-2.0 licenses respectively, with software based on the excellent Blueretro.</p>
