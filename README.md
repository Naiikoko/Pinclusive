<p align="justify">Pinclusive is a multiplayer wired and Bluetooth controller adapter for Pinball Machines, built on the popular ESP32 chip. It has been designed to allow people with disabilities to enjoy playing Pinball. It allows connection of multiple controllers and gamepads as listed below, keyboards and mice over bluetooth, as well as wired buttons designed for disabilities such as the Logitech Adaptive kit. Pinclusive supports up to 4 simultaneous devices, for multiplayer games !</p>
<p align="justify">Simply use the Bluetooth devices you already own!  Xbox Adaptive Controller (XAC), Quadstick, Wii, Switch, PS3, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S & generic HID Bluetooth (BR/EDR & LE) devices are supported.</p>
<br>
<p align="justify">The present repository relates to the firmware of the Pinclusive Hardware, based on ESP32.</p>
<p align="justify">A further repository is being created : Pinclusive Webconfig. Coming up soon !</p>
